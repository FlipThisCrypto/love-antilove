# Style Reference Notes

The current visual target, based on `sample1` through `sample8`, is:

- ornate chibi fantasy wizard, not a plain block sprite
- oversized decorated hat with heart jewels, hanging charms, and curved silhouette
- huge heart or broken-heart staff carried beside the body
- full-body centered character with tiny boots and readable pose
- layered robe with dense trim, gems, chains, bows, bottles, skulls, hearts, or rose details
- cute familiar near the feet, usually cat, bat, bird, or small magical pet
- transparent or near-transparent sticker-style character render
- LOVE palette: white, blush pink, lavender, warm gold, soft rose glow
- ANTILOVE palette: black, charcoal, crimson, magenta, purple, gothic rose accents
- magical particles around the character, but no frame, card UI, logo, watermark, captions, numbers, or readable text

Some references include signs or labels, but the collection rule remains no text in the image. Convert those ideas into textless charms, blank plaques, bottles, skull props, broken hearts, and symbolic accessories.
